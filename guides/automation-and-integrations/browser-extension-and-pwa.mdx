# Saving Links from Your Browser

Linkwarden empowers you to save, organize, and preserve web content effortlessly through its browser extension and installable Progressive Web App (PWA). This guide walks you through integrating Linkwarden into your daily browsing routine, enabling quick saving of links, direct content sending, and highlighting text—all without interrupting your workflow.

---

## 1. Overview

Linkwarden's browser extension and PWA are designed to capture valuable web content instantly while you browse. Whether you're researching, reading, or curating, you can save links and segments of webpages directly to your Linkwarden account. This tight integration transforms your casual browsing into an organized, searchable knowledge base.

### Why Use Linkwarden's Browser Extension and PWA?

- **Speedy Saving:** Quickly add links to Linkwarden with one click.
- **In-Browser Highlighting:** Save important excerpts by highlighting text.
- **Direct Content Sending:** Send pages or selected content to Linkwarden without leaving your browser or mobile app.
- **Cross-Platform Consistency:** Use the extension on desktop or the PWA on mobile for seamless access.

---

## 2. Prerequisites

Before proceeding, ensure:

- You have a registered Linkwarden account and are logged in.
- You have installed the Linkwarden browser extension compatible with your preferred browser or have access to the Linkwarden PWA on your mobile device.

If you need setup instructions for the browser extension or PWA installation, refer to the [Installation Methods](https://docs.linkwarden.app/getting-started/prerequisites-installation/installation-methods).

---

## 3. Getting Started with the Browser Extension

Follow these steps to install the browser extension and start saving links:

<Steps>
<Step title="Install the Extension">
- Visit the official extension store for your browser (e.g., Chrome Web Store, Firefox Add-ons).
- Search for 'Linkwarden' and click 'Add to browser' to install.
- After installation, confirm the extension icon appears next to your address bar.
</Step>
<Step title="Authenticate the Extension">
- Click the Linkwarden extension icon.
- Log in using your Linkwarden credentials if prompted.
- Once logged in, the extension is connected to your account.
</Step>
<Step title="Save a Link">
- While browsing, click the extension icon on any webpage.
- Edit the link's title or description if desired.
- Assign collections or tags to organize your saved link immediately.
- Click 'Save' to add the link to your Linkwarden.
</Step>
<Step title="Highlight and Save Text">
- Select text on any webpage.
- Right-click and choose 'Save selection to Linkwarden' from the context menu.
- The excerpt is saved as part of a new link entry for easy retrieval.
</Step>
</Steps>

### Expected Outcome

After completing these steps, your saved links and selected text appear in your Linkwarden dashboard, preserved automatically for future access.

---

## 4. Using the Linkwarden Progressive Web App (PWA)

The Linkwarden PWA provides a mobile-friendly, installable app experience accessible from your browser without app store installation delays.

### Installing the PWA

1. Open your mobile browser and navigate to your Linkwarden web application.
2. Look for the PWA installation prompt that appears near the bottom or in your browser menu.
3. Tap 'Install' to add Linkwarden to your home screen as an app.

<Tip>
The PWA offers offline access to your saved links and integrates with your mobile device's share functionality for rapid link saving.
</Tip>

### Saving Links via PWA

- Use the device's share menu to send URLs directly to the Linkwarden PWA.
- Highlight text within the PWA to add notes or excerpts for better context.
- Organize saved content into collections and assign tags via the app interface.

---

## 5. Best Practices for Efficient Saving

- **Organize Immediately:** When saving, assign collections and tags to avoid clutter.
- **Use Highlights:** Save relevant excerpts to capture important information quickly.
- **Leverage PWA Share Features:** On mobile, use the native share option to send links without switching apps.
- **Stay Signed In:** Keep your browser extension and PWA logged in for swift saving without re-authentication.

## 6. Troubleshooting Common Issues

<AccordionGroup title="Troubleshooting Saving Issues">
<Accordion title="Extension Not Saving Links">
- Ensure you are logged in through the extension.
- Refresh the browser tab and try again.
- Disable conflicting extensions that may block scripts.
- If the problem persists, reinstall the extension.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="PWA Installation Prompt Not Showing">
- Confirm your browser supports PWAs (Chrome, Edge, Firefox mobile).
- Clear your browser cache.
- Access Linkwarden over HTTPS.
- Manually add to home screen via browser menu.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Highlighted Text Not Saving">
- Check that the right-click context menu has the Linkwarden save option.
- Verify the extension has permission to interact with webpage content.
- Restart the browser or device.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## 7. Tips for Maximizing Browser Integration

- On desktop, use keyboard shortcuts (if available) for faster saves.
- Regularly review saved links in your dashboard to maintain organization.
